王晟宁，[大连理工大学](https://www.dlut.edu.cn)[机械工程学院](https://me.dlut.edu.cn)博士在读，现于[高性能精密制造全国重点实验室](https://me.dlut.edu.cn)开展研究，师从[宋学官教授](https://faculty.dlut.edu.cn/sxg/zh_CN/index.htm)。2022—2026 年就读于大连理工大学机械设计制造及其自动化专业，获工学学士学位。

研究方向为面向复杂工程系统的科学机器学习（SciML），具体包括神经算子的时空建模与几何泛化。

---

I am a Ph.D. student at the [State Key Laboratory of High-performance Precision Manufacturing](https://me.dlut.edu.cn), [School of Mechanical Engineering](https://me.dlut.edu.cn), [Dalian University of Technology](https://www.dlut.edu.cn), advised by [Prof. Xueguan Song](https://faculty.dlut.edu.cn/sxg/zh_CN/index.htm). Before starting my doctoral studies, I earned a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from Dalian University of Technology (2022–2026).

My research focuses on scientific machine learning (SciML) for complex engineering systems. I am particularly interested in neural operators for spatio-temporal modeling and geometric generalization.
