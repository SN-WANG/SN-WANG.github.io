I am a Ph.D. student in the [State Key Laboratory of High-performance Precision Manufacturing, School of Mechanical Engineering](https://me.dlut.edu.cn) at [Dalian University of Technology](https://www.dlut.edu.cn), advised by [Prof. Xueguan Song](https://faculty.dlut.edu.cn/sxg/zh_CN/index.htm). I received my Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from Dalian University of Technology in 2026 (2022-2026).

My long-term goal is to develop scientific machine learning methods that make the prediction and design of complex engineering systems more accurate and efficient. I am particularly interested in neural operators for spatio-temporal dynamics and variable-geometry problems, with an emphasis on translating these methods to complex engineering equipment.
