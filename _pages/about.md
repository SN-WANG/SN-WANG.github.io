I am a Ph.D. student in the [State Key Laboratory of High-performance Precision Manufacturing, School of Mechanical Engineering](https://me.dlut.edu.cn) at [Dalian University of Technology](https://www.dlut.edu.cn), advised by [Prof. Xueguan Song](https://faculty.dlut.edu.cn/sxg/zh_CN/index.htm). Before starting my doctoral studies, I earned a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from Dalian University of Technology (2022–2026).

My research focuses on scientific machine learning (SciML) for complex engineering systems. I am particularly interested in neural operators for spatio-temporal modeling and geometric generalization.
